Introduction

We are going to develop an app that provides the smarter functioning for library.

This app contains two versions


First is “Paid Version”

PAID VERSION::

This version offers students faculty approved books at their door step by third party deliver app.

Students enrolled in this app with their name,collage and resident address get books at deliverd at home which approved by their respective collage faculties.


And the second is “Free Version”

FREE VERSION::

This version offers student to get books from their seniors by paying senior student.

This version solves the problem of getting contacted with their collage seniors.

Abstract

Many students find it difficult to get perfect book from local store.


SOME PROBLEMS FACED BY STUDENTS:

1> Not getting required edition of book.

2> Have to pay some extra amount to buy fresh books from local store because of less stock.

3> Getting damaged book while buying used books.

4> Some pages lost in used books.


So we decide to solve this problem of students by developing this app.

If student buy new book from our app he or she gets particular edition used by their faculties.

And if student buy used books from seniors using this app we provides verified book feature to not get damaged or lost page book.
